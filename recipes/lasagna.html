grandma's lasagna

Here's a rich, hearty, and delicious lasagna recipe for you. It features layers of tender pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta filling, and plenty of melted mozzarella.

Each bite has this comforting, savory depth, with just a hint of warmth from the red pepper flakes.
